About

I'm a journalist and Fulbright Scholar with expertise in investigative reporting, data-driven journalism, and health policy coverage. Currently based in the Washington D.C. area, I work as a correspondent for Reuters News covering the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries.

My work has contributed to Pulitzer Prize-winning investigations at The Washington Post, where I helped maintain and update critical databases tracking police-involved incidents. I've also worked with Voice of America on press freedom issues and generated extensive coverage of higher education policy for International Business Times.

I hold a Master's degree in Journalism and Public Affairs from American University and completed my Fulbright exchange program at West Liberty University, where I achieved Dean's List distinction. I'm fluent in English, Hindi, and Marathi, bringing a global perspective to my reporting.

Skills: Investigative journalism, data analysis, Reuters Terminal, FOIA requests, court document analysis, multilingual reporting, stock market coverage, healthcare policy, database management, social media strategy.
